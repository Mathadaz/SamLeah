Who We Are

We Don't Just Plan Events.
We Craft Stories.

SamLeah was born from a simple belief — that every gathering, no matter its occasion, deserves to be honoured with intention and grace. Whether it's a room full of laughter or a room full of tears, we show up fully prepared to serve you.

Our team of experienced coordinators, photographers, and MCs ensure that from the first moment to the last, you are present — while we handle every detail.
