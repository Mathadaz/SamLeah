FAQs

Questions We're Often Asked

How far in advance should I book?↓
For weddings, we recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance. For parties and other events, 4–8 weeks is usually sufficient. For urgent bookings, contact us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
Do you handle funerals and memorial services with respect?↓
Absolutely. Funerals and memorials are sacred occasions and we treat them with the utmost dignity. Our team is trained to be compassionate, unobtrusive, and professional throughout, honouring both the deceased and the family.
Are you available outside of Gauteng?↓
Yes! We are based in Tshaulu, Masiwane and regularly serve both Gauteng and Limpopo. Travel to other provinces can be arranged — please mention your location when you enquire.
Can I book photography only, without full coordination?↓
Yes. Our photography and MC services are available as standalone bookings. You don't need to take the full package — we'll tailor our offering to exactly what you need.
Do you work with cultural and traditional ceremonies?↓
Yes, proudly. We understand and honour South African cultural traditions — from Venda lobola ceremonies to Zulu weddings and everything in between. Our team includes multilingual hosts who can conduct programmes in your preferred language.
What is your payment structure?↓
We typically require a deposit to secure your date, with the balance due closer to the event. Exact terms are outlined in our service agreement, which we'll share after your initial consultation.
