Who We Are

More Than an Events Company. We're Memory Makers.

SamLeah Holdings was founded on a deeply personal truth — that the most meaningful moments of our lives deserve to be handled with care, skill, and heart. Whether a room is filled with joy or grief, we believe every gathering is sacred.

What started as a small operation in Tshaulu, Masiwane has grown into a trusted name across Gauteng. We've had the privilege of running weddings where tears of happiness fell, birthday parties where laughter rang through the night, and memorial services where dignity and love were held in equal measure.

We don't just plan events — we step in fully, so you can step back and simply be present.
